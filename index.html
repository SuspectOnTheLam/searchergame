Searcher is a mystery set in North America about a First Nation's unsolved murder.
The year is 1742.

You are a man named Hiawatha who is tasked to fight off the evil brought on by the unjust death of Natarisha, The Great Lord’s son. Long ago, your ancestors lived in peace and harmony.

Unfortunately, this was all to end. Malsumis, the trickster god, ever jealous of his older brother Glooscap, had cornered his nephew, Natarisha, onto a cliff.
The world deteriorated, fights broke out, and clans split apart. Brother turned on brother and chaos ensued. It's your time now Hiawatha.

You have to rise to the challenge. You have to face your fears and make this world a beautiful one for all of us.
